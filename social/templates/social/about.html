Kasubay Campus Connect

About Kasubay Campus Connect

Our Mission

Kasubay Campus Connect is dedicated to fostering meaningful connections among students, faculty, and alumni across campuses. We provide a secure platform for sharing knowledge, resources, and experiences that enhance campus life.

What We Offer

  • Student-led communities for every interest and department
  • Secure private groups for classes and organizations
  • Campus event coordination and promotion
  • Resource sharing and academic collaboration tools
  • Alumni networking opportunities

Our Values

Inclusivity

We welcome students from all backgrounds and foster diverse, respectful communities.

Integrity

We maintain transparency in our operations and protect user privacy.

Innovation

We continuously improve our platform to meet evolving campus needs.

Community

We believe in the power of connection to enhance the educational experience.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2024

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when creating an account, joining communities, or interacting with others:

  • Personal identification (name, email, institution)
  • Academic information (major, year)
  • Content you post or share
  • Messages and communications
  • Technical data (IP address, device information)

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data helps us:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Verify academic status
  • Personalize your experience
  • Facilitate campus-specific features
  • Ensure community safety and compliance

3. Data Protection

We implement industry-standard measures to protect your information:

  • End-to-end encryption for private messages
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited data retention policies
  • Strict access controls

4. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and request your data
  • Correct inaccuracies
  • Request deletion of your account
  • Opt-out of data processing

Contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@earaz.ca@gmail.com for any requests.
